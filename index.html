We believe that when you eat fresh, you think fresh; and we bring this fresh thinking in all our endeavours. We
are committed to innovating better, healthier, high-quality food that delights our customers. We are dedicated to
fostering positive impact by providing sustainable livelihoods to our suppliers and meaningful careers to our
team.


Mr. sandwich India Pvt Limited (EIPL) has the master franchise rights to open and operate mr.sandwich restaurants in
India, Sri Lanka and Bangladesh. Mr. sandwich is set to expand freshness and power through the mission of serving
healthy, tasty, and fresh food to everyone.


India is the largest market for EIPL and is focussed to expand Mr. sandwich footprint aggressively. EIPL is a fully
owned subsidiary of Culinary Brands Private Limited.
